My Writing

In my opinion, 21st century world leadership should be based on wisdom rather than power. Power-based leadership focuses only on force and control, but wise leadership pursues cooperation and mutual understanding. First, wise leaders listen to others and make decisions that benefit everyone. Second, they use communication skills to resolve conflicts peacefully. Therefore, I believe that a leader who leads with wisdom is more effective and trustworthy in today's complex world.

Corrections

In my opinion, 21st century world leadership should be based on wisdom rather than power. Power-based leadership focuses solely on force and control, but wise leadership pursues cooperation and mutual understanding. Furthermore, wise leaders listen to others carefully and make decisions that benefit everyone. In contrast, they use communication skills to resolve conflicts peacefully. Therefore, I believe that a leader who leads with wisdom is more effective and reliable in today's intricate world.

Explanation

  • focuses only on → focuses solely on : "only" 대신 "solely"를 사용하면 더 격식 있는 표현이 됩니다.
  • First / Second → Furthermore / In contrast : 단순 나열 대신 비교·대조를 드러내는 연결어로 교체하면 비교문 구조가 명확해집니다.
  • trustworthy → reliable : 문맥상 "reliable"이 리더십의 신뢰성을 더 직접적으로 표현합니다.
  • complex world → intricate world : "intricate"은 "complex"보다 어휘 수준이 높아 글의 다양성을 높여줍니다.

My Writing

In my opinion, 21st century world leadership should be based on wisdom rather than power. Power-based leadership focuses solely on force and control, but wise leadership pursues cooperation and mutual understanding. Furthermore, wise leaders listen to others carefully and make decisions that benefit everyone. In contrast, power-based leaders often ignore the voices of others. Second, they use communication skills to resolve conflicts peacefully. Therefore, I strongly believe that a leader who leads with wisdom is more effective and reliable in today's intricate world.

Corrections

In my opinion, 21st century world leadership should be based on wisdom rather than power. Power-based leadership focuses solely on force and control, but wise leadership pursues cooperation and mutual understanding. Furthermore, wise leaders listen to others carefully and make decisions that are beneficial to all. In contrast, power-based leaders often ignore the voices of others. Additionally, they use communication skills to resolve conflicts peacefully. Therefore, I firmly believe that a leader who leads with wisdom is more effective and reliable in today's intricate world.

Explanation

  • make decisions that benefit everyone → make decisions that are beneficial to all :
  • Second → Additionally : 앞 문장이 이미 대조(In contrast)로 시작했으므로, 추가 논점은 "Additionally"로 이어가는 것이 자연스럽습니다.
  • strongly believe → firmly believe : "firmly"는 "strongly"보다 격식체 글쓰기에 더 적합한 표현입니다.

Final Writing Submission

In my opinion, 21st century world leadership should be based on wisdom rather than power. Power-based leadership focuses solely on force and control, but wise leadership pursues cooperation and mutual understanding. Furthermore, wise leaders listen to others carefully and make decisions that are beneficial to all. In contrast, power-based leaders often ignore the voices of others. Additionally, wise leaders use communication skills to resolve conflicts peacefully. Therefore, I firmly believe that a leader who leads with wisdom is more effective and reliable in today's intricate world.
